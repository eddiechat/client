# Ideas for AI skills shared through the marketplace

## Personal Productivity
- Smart Follow-up - Flags conversations awaiting replies and drafts gentle nudges
- Meeting Scheduler - Proposes available times based on calendar and negotiates back-and-forth
- Newsletter Digest - Consolidates subscriptions into a single daily/weekly summary
- Travel Extractor - Pulls flight, hotel, and booking details into calendar events
- Receipt Filer - Identifies invoices and receipts, extracts key data, organizes by category
- Unsubscribe Assistant - Identifies unwanted subscriptions and handles opt-outs

## Communication
- Translator - Real-time translation of incoming and outgoing messages
- Tone Adjuster - Rewrites drafts to be more formal, casual, or concise
- Thread Summarizer - Condenses long email chains into key points and action items
- Reply Suggester - Offers quick response options based on message context

## Professional & Team
- Meeting Notes Extractor - Parses meeting summaries and distributes action items
- Intro Connector - Drafts double opt-in introductions between contacts
- Out-of-Office Responder - Context-aware auto-replies that answer common questions
- Contract Flagger - Highlights emails containing deadlines, legal terms, or signature requests
- Job Application Tracker - Monitors application status and follow-up timing

## Safety & Privacy
- Phishing Detector - Warns about suspicious links, sender spoofing, and social engineering
- Sensitive Data Scanner - Flags outgoing messages containing passwords, IDs, or financial info

## Creative & Content
- Blog-to-Newsletter - Reformats long-form content for email distribution
- Social Draft Generator - Creates post drafts from email announcements or updates
- Birthday & Anniversary Reminder - Extracts dates from signatures and past messages, prompts timely notes
